About Us

Serving Mosobeti with compassion, excellence, and integrity.

Oasis Maternity & Nursing Home

Where Every Life Begins with Care

History: Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti was established to fill the gap in quality maternal and nursing care in the region. Founded by a team of passionate healthcare professionals, the facility has grown to become a trusted name in community health.

Why We Were Established: To ensure every mother, child, and family in Mosobeti has access to affordable, high-quality healthcare delivered with dignity and respect.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services that enhance the health and well-being of mothers, children, and the entire community through professional excellence and patient-centered care.

Our Vision

To be the leading maternity and nursing healthcare provider in Mosobeti and beyond, recognized for excellence in maternal health services, innovation in healthcare delivery, and unwavering commitment to compassionate care.

Health Tips

Empowering you with knowledge for better health.

Importance of Antenatal Care

Regular ANC visits help detect complications early, ensuring a healthy pregnancy and safe delivery.

Newborn Care Tips

Keep your baby warm, breastfeed within the first hour, and practice kangaroo care.

Breastfeeding Guidance

Exclusive breastfeeding for six months provides optimal nutrition and immunity.

Family Planning Methods

Explore contraceptive options — from pills to IUDs — to make informed choices.

Vaccination Schedules

Follow the national immunization schedule to protect against preventable diseases.

Nutrition During Pregnancy

Eat a balanced diet rich in iron, calcium, and folic acid. Stay hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries.

What should I bring for delivery?

ID, antenatal records, baby clothes, diapers, sanitary pads, toiletries, and comfortable clothing.

How do I book an appointment?

Use the appointment form on this page, call us, or visit our reception.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we have a 24/7 emergency response team. Call +254 712 345 678.

What are your operating hours?

Mon–Fri: 7AM–8PM, Sat–Sun: 8AM–6PM. Emergency services 24/7.

Do you accept NHIF?

Yes, we accept NHIF and other major insurance providers.

Can I visit the maternity ward?

Visiting hours: 10AM–12PM and 3PM–5PM daily.
