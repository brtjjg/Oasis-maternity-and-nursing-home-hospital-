About Us

Serving Mosobeti with compassion, excellence, and integrity.

Oasis Maternity & Nursing Home

Where Every Life Begins with Care

History: Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti was established to fill the gap in quality maternal and nursing care in the region. Founded by a team of passionate healthcare professionals, the facility has grown to become a trusted name in community health.

Why We Were Established: To ensure every mother, child, and family in Mosobeti has access to affordable, high-quality healthcare delivered with dignity and respect.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services that enhance the health and well-being of mothers, children, and the entire community through professional excellence and patient-centered care.

Our Vision

To be the leading maternity and nursing healthcare provider in Mosobeti and beyond, recognized for excellence in maternal health services, innovation in healthcare delivery, and unwavering commitment to compassionate care.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti Privacy Policy

Introduction

Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti is committed to protecting the privacy, dignity, and confidentiality of all patients, visitors, employees, and users of our services. We recognize that healthcare information is highly sensitive and must be handled responsibly, securely, and ethically.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose personal information obtained through our healthcare services, hospital management systems, website, mobile applications, and other communication channels.

By accessing our services, visiting our facility, or using our digital platforms, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information
Full Name, National ID or Passport Number, Date of Birth, Gender, Physical Address, Phone Number, Email Address, Emergency Contact Information

Medical Information
Medical History, Previous Diagnoses, Laboratory Results, Prescriptions, Treatment Records, Immunization Records, Pregnancy and Maternal Health Records, Hospital Admission Records

Financial Information
Payment Records, Insurance Information, M-PESA Transaction References, Billing History

System Information
Login Activity, IP Addresses, Device Information, User Access Logs, Security Audit Trails

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: Register patients, schedule appointments, provide medical treatment, maintain medical records, process payments, generate prescriptions, conduct laboratory testing, communicate appointment reminders, improve healthcare services, meet legal and regulatory requirements, prevent fraud and unauthorized access.

Confidentiality of Medical Records

Patient records are strictly confidential. Medical information will only be accessible to authorized healthcare personnel involved in patient care, including: Doctors, Nurses, Laboratory Technologists, Pharmacists, Hospital Administrators (where necessary).

Unauthorized access, sharing, or disclosure of patient information is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action or legal consequences.

Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including: Encrypted data storage, Secure authentication systems, Role-based access controls, Password protection, Automatic session timeouts, Security monitoring, Audit logging, Regular system backups.

All users are required to maintain the confidentiality of their login credentials.

Retention of Information

Medical and administrative records are retained according to applicable healthcare regulations and professional standards. Records may be archived for future reference, legal compliance, healthcare continuity, and operational purposes.

Patient Rights Regarding Privacy

Patients have the right to: Access their personal information, Request corrections where legally permitted, Receive explanations regarding data usage, Raise privacy concerns, Receive protection against unauthorized disclosures.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal, technological, or operational changes.

Terms and Conditions of Service

👥 Patient Rights

Patient Rights and Responsibilities Charter

Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti recognizes and respects the rights of every patient.

Right to Respect and Dignity

Every patient shall be treated with: Courtesy, Compassion, Respect, Professionalism – without discrimination based on: Age, Gender, Religion, Disability, Social Status, Ethnicity.

Right to Information

Patients have the right to receive clear explanations regarding: Diagnosis, Treatment options, Procedures, Risks and benefits, Costs and billing. Information shall be provided in a manner understandable to the patient.

Right to Privacy

Patients have the right to confidentiality regarding: Medical records, Consultations, Treatment plans, Personal information.

Right to Consent

Patients have the right to make informed decisions regarding treatment. No procedure shall be performed without appropriate consent except in emergency situations permitted by law.

Right to Safe Care

Patients have the right to receive: Competent healthcare, Safe treatment, Quality services, Clean facilities.

Right to Raise Complaints

Patients may report concerns regarding: Service quality, Staff conduct, Billing issues, Facility conditions. All complaints shall be investigated fairly and professionally.

🛡 Data Protection

Data Protection Commitment

Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti is committed to protecting personal and medical information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, loss, or destruction.

Data Classification

Public Data – General information available to the public.
Internal Data – Operational information used by staff.
Confidential Data – Patient records, billing information, and healthcare documentation.

Access Control

Access to information is granted according to staff responsibilities. Examples: Receptionists access registration information, Nurses access nursing records, Doctors access clinical records, Pharmacists access prescriptions, Cashiers access billing records, Administrators manage system operations.

No employee shall access information unrelated to their duties.

Audit Trails

Every activity performed within the hospital system is recorded. Examples include: Patient registration, Diagnosis updates, Prescription creation, Payment processing, Laboratory result entry. The system records: User Name, Department, Date, Time, Activity Performed. This ensures accountability and transparency.

Security Monitoring

The hospital continuously monitors systems for: Unauthorized access attempts, Suspicious activities, Data breaches, Security vulnerabilities. Appropriate action shall be taken when threats are identified.

Backup and Recovery

Regular backups are performed to ensure: Data availability, Business continuity, Disaster recovery. Backups are securely stored and periodically tested.

Breach Reporting

Any suspected security incident must be reported immediately to hospital management for investigation and corrective action.

Commitment Statement

Oasis Maternity and Nursing Home Mosobeti remains committed to providing healthcare services in a manner that protects patient rights, safeguards personal information, promotes transparency, and upholds the highest standards of professional ethics and integrity.
